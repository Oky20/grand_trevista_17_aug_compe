How to Join
1
Register via Google Form
Make sure you have submitted the registration form with your Strava profile link. Only registered participants can join.
2
Connect your Strava account
Click "Connect with Strava" on the leaderboard page and authorize the app. Your team will be assigned automatically based on your registration.
3
Sync your activities
After connecting, click "Sync" to pull your activities from Strava. Data syncs automatically every day at midnight.
4
Check the leaderboard
Your points will appear on the leaderboard after syncing. Check the Scoring Guide tab to see how points are calculated.
Frequently Asked Questions
I get "Not Registered" when connecting Strava.
Your Strava ID is not in the participant list yet. Make sure you submitted the registration form, then contact admin to get added. Share your Strava profile URL (e.g. strava.com/athletes/12345678).
My activities are not showing up.
Click the "Sync" button after connecting. If activities still don't appear, make sure your Strava activities are set to Public or Followers. Private activities cannot be accessed.
My points seem incorrect.
Check the Activities tab to see point breakdown per activity. Note that activities below the minimum duration for your sport type, or with less than 4 kcal/min, do not earn points.
I'm in the wrong team.
Team assignment is based on your registration data. If there's an error, contact the admin to correct it.
How often is the leaderboard updated?
Automatically every day at midnight (WIB). You can also manually sync anytime by clicking the "Sync" button after logging in.
Can I disconnect my Strava account?
Yes — click "Sign out" on the leaderboard. To fully revoke access, go to your Strava settings → My Apps, and remove GTR Fest 17. Contact admin if you want your data deleted.
